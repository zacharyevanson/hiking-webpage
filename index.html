General information...

Cape Town was recently named Time Out's 'Best City in the World 2025', and there is no better time to start exploring the city's wonders. Among its most impressive features are the mountains that surround the beautiful city. From Table Mountain to Lion's Head, there are countless trails and tracks for hikers to explore. Most notable of these (and included in this website) are:

  • Lion's Head - A popular sunrise and sunset hike with a short but steep climb with rewarding 360° views
  • Devil's Peak - A less crowded but rewarding trail offering fantastic views of the city
  • Kloof Corner - A short but scenic trail with a mindblowing lookout perfect for sunsets
  • Skeleton Gorge - A lush trail beginning in Kirstenbosch Botanical and leading to the peak of Table mountain

These are among some of the most popular routes nestled in the Cape mountains, but there are many more less known ones, all offering a different view to showcase Cape Town's natural beauty.
