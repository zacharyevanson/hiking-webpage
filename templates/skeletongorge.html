Skeleton Gorge

About...

Found on Table Mountain, Skeleton Gorge is a wonderful but challenging hike that has always been popular with some of the more experienced hikers. Initially, it consists of a single rocky track that rises into the lush Table Mountain foliage. The route requires some light scrambling, and there are a couple ladders to help you up as well.

After making your way through the beautiful Kirstenbosch Botanical, the hike begins in a very dense plant environment. It then leads all the way up Table Mountain, with a 'beach' at the head of the trail. This is one of the major attractions of the hike, alongwith a waterfall, and an abundance of local biodiversity.
