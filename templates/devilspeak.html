Devil's Peak

About...

Poking out next to Table Mountain is Devil's Peak, a less popular but equally iconic mountain, offering some extraordinary trails. Riddled with fynbos and other indigenous life, the hikes here offer natural beauty and incredible views of Cape Town's city centre. This hike is scalable for most, including dogs and kids!

There are various trails that split in a fork right at the beginning of the trail, but they all lead back to the beginning so its difficult to get lost.
