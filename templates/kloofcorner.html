Kloof Corner

About...

Kloof Corner is definitely the easiest hike on this list, but still not to be underestimated. There are a number of steeper sections on this hike that will make you sweat. Also, there is a lot of loose rock, so just take care when placing feet and balance. Otherwise, this is an excellent and super rewarding hike, with views of the city bowl, Camps Bay, Lion's Head, all overlooking the massive Atlantic Ocean.

A shorter but very sweet hike that offers incredible lookouts of Cape Town, this is very much a local and non-local favourite. It is short enough to do before or after work, and still feel like you've enjoyed a thorough workout.
